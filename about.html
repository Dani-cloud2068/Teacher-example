About Us

About Us Welcome to Heritage Vineyards, where tradition meets innovation in every bottle. Our journey began over three decades ago, nestled in the sunlit hills of Napa Valley, with a dream of crafting exceptional wines that tell a story. Our Story In 1992, Robert and Clara Donovan, a passionate couple with deep roots in viticulture, embarked on a mission to create a winery that celebrated both the art of winemaking and the beauty of nature. Robert, a former agronomist, and Clara, a talented artist with a penchant for storytelling, combined their unique skills to lay the foundation for what would become Heritage Vineyards. Initially, our vineyard started as a modest plot of land with just a handful of grapevines. Robert meticulously chose varietals that thrived in the local terroir, while Clara designed our first labels, reflecting the elegance and richness of the wines. The early years were a labor of love, with the Donovans working tirelessly to perfect their craft and build a legacy. By the early 2000s, Heritage Vineyards began to gain recognition for its distinctive blends and commitment to quality. The turning point came in 2004 when our flagship Cabernet Sauvignon received its first prestigious award. This accolade put us on the map and sparked a period of growth and innovation. In 2010, the Donovans passed the torch to their children, Emma and James, who brought fresh perspectives and new ideas to the family business. Emma, with her background in environmental science, introduced sustainable farming practices, ensuring our vineyards flourished while preserving the land for future generations. James, a skilled sommelier, expanded our portfolio with exciting new varietals and unique blends that pushed the boundaries of traditional winemaking. Today, Heritage Vineyards is celebrated not only for its exceptional wines but also for its dedication to sustainable practices and community involvement. Our state-of-the-art winery is a testament to our commitment to blending tradition with innovation. We continue to honor the legacy of Robert and Clara by crafting wines that are as rich in history as they are in flavor.
